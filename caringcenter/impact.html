Our Impact

When women are in the vulnerable position of an unplanned pregnancy, a helping hand can make an enormous impact. Here at the Women’s Care Center, we’re proud to support and uplift pregnant women, helping them gain the knowledge and confidence that they need to succeed.

Pregnant women visit our center for a free pregnancy test or ultrasound. In addition to these basic pregnancy services, our patients develop close, trusting relationships with our counselors. It’s these personal connections that make the support of the Women’s Care Center invaluable in a young woman’s life.

For most, the choice of life takes time.

Through counseling, mentoring, education, and valuable resources, women can work towards the decision for life. This courageous choice isn’t easy for many of our clients, but we’re proud to provide them with the tools to make a confident decision.

Your donations make our work possible.

With your generous support of our center, we’re able to care for vulnerable women as they navigate the uncertainty of an unplanned pregnancy. Through encouragement and care, over 90% of our clients choose life. And it’s all possible thanks to donors like you!
