Understanding The CMMC

The CMMC, or Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification, is the standard for cybersecurity implementation in the DIB (Defense Industrial Base). In September, 2020, the DoD began to phase CMMC certification in, and it will gradually become a requirement for DIB participants and DoD contractors.

As a response to cybersecurity shortcomings in the DIB, the CMMC serves to regulate cybersecurity practices among contractors and protect sensitive information.
