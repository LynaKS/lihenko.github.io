Why Join Baker Cardiology?

The concierge model allows patients to access exceptional heart care in an easy, relaxed and timely manner. It’s like having a cardiologist with you at all times.

Join now

Benefits Not Found in a Traditional Cardiology Care Model

  • 24/7 direct communication with Dr. Baker via phone, text, or email
  • Calls answered by Dr. Baker in a timely manner
  • Same-day or next-day appointments available
  • Little to no wait time
  • Unhurried office visits in a relaxed, comfortable setting
  • Limited practice size, which allows for more time and communication
  • Video visits available, possibly eliminating need for trip to office or the ER
  • Home visits for medically appropriate situations
  • Dr. Baker is in direct communication with providers across the spectrum of care
  • Direct communication of lab and test results for your peace of mind
  • Emphasis on health coaching and wellness education
  • Prevention is key focus

Other Services Provided by Baker Cardiology

  • Dr. Baker acts as your health advocate
  • Coordination of care for tests, treatments and hospitalizations
  • If you are hospitalized, Dr. Baker is available to assist you with treatment decisions
  • If you need to be seen in the Emergency Department for a heart-related issue Dr. Baker will contact the emergency room physician and hospitalist to discuss your plan of care
