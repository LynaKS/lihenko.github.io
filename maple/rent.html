Introduction:

Perfect for weddings, corporate or special events with stunning views of the Endless Mountain. The Main house is a 7.500 square foot Palladio style inspired residence build from property field stone with wood siding to match the landscape. It is located at the top of the property, overlooking the fifty-acre farm.

There is a beautiful large deck surrounding the house. Perfect for cocktail or dinner receptions or wedding ceremonies. Across from the entrance on an upper level, hidden by lush landscaping next to the main house is a large swimming pool area with a pool house cabana. This unique estate is surrounded by lovely mature trees and shrubs, patio and a beautiful great size swimming pool. This unique estate has a lot of history.

The property has large waterfalls. Leonard's Creek runs through the estate. Most of the land is surrounded by field stone walls and mature trees.
