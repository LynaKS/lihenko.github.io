Amenities

At 50ACF, you can create your perfect event day by choosing from one of our all-inclusive services or non-inclusive options. From our historic barn as the main space to the numerous add-on areas you can choose from, 50ACF has a great foundation for building your dream day.

Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, many weddings and other significant events originally planned for 2020 were postponed or canceled. However, with Pennsylvania lifting nearly all COVID-19 restrictions on Memorial Day 2021, now is the time to start planning gatherings again.

At 50ACF, you will work directly with our staff and vendors who know our location and how to best manage your event. We have worked with numerous local catering services and party planning companies who can help you create the event of your dreams. As every event is different, we also understand if you would like to supply your own food, beverages, and decorations.

Rustic Barn:

Our historic barn event space has a two-level main barn that measures 45 ft x 50 ft on both levels. The main barn was recently fully renovated by an Amish master carpenter. The space holds up to 200 Guests inside. Included in this event space is a large outdoor area, waterfall, and a smaller covered space at the entrance of the property that is perfect for food, drinks, or music.

For an additional fee, you can also rent or bring your own tents to set up in the outdoor area

50ACF Estate provides onsite parking for all guests, as well as a valet service if desired.

Modern Dwelling:

Our newly built large event dwelling is perfect for smaller gatherings and holds up to 120 people. This space has a large first floor as well as an upper-level entertainment space, both of which have A/C and heating. Further, the first floor of this event space has a unique open fireplace that creates a cozy atmosphere.

With large folding doors on both the front and back of the building along with windows spanning both sides, this space invites a light breeze and helps to create a perfect country feeling that brings the outdoors inside.

Included in this package is our swimming pool area and Cabana.
