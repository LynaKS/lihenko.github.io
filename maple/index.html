Welcome

At the 50 Acre Farm Estate (50ACF), you will experience the rolling hills and picturesque countryside North Eastern Pennsylvania is known for. However, the following distinct property features are what have drawn small to large gatherings to 50ACF for over two decades:

  • Recently renovated rustic barn event space
  • A newly built two story event space
  • A one-of-a-kind waterfall that runs through the front of the property
  • Original stonewalls surrounding the entrance of the estate
  • Lush well-kept greenery throughout the property
  • A pool and connected cabana
  • Stunning views of the valley and surrounding mountains

These unique details and more are why 50ACF hosts numerous wedding ceremonies and receptions, birthday parties, corporate outings, retirement parties, family celebrations, and other gatherings every year.

Sitting between Dallas PA and Beaumont PA on Route 309 North at the corner of Falls Hill Road, 50 Acre Farm Estate offers a perfect blend of rustic style and convenience for your special occasion. With two separate event spaces each capable of hosting 120 – 200 guests and x different event packages, we have options every type of event and budget.

Established in 1863

The 50 Acre Farm Estate (WWW.50ACF.com), established in the mid-1800s is a more than one-hundred-and-fifty-year-old farm with waterfalls and creeks situated on 50 private acres. Before it was purchased by the current owner of the property, it was a local dairy farm that supplied much of the surrounding region for over a century. This historic farmstead is registered with the National Register Program, Bureau for Historic Preservation in Harrisburg PA. First mentioned in the Civil War Draft Registration in 1863, the Farm is a historic American protected asset.
