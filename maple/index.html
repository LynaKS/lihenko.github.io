Welcome

At the 50 Acre Farm Estate (50ACF), you will experience the rolling hills and picturesque countryside North Eastern Pennsylvania is known for. However, the following distinct property features are what have drawn small to large gatherings to 50ACF for over two decades:

  • Recently renovated rustic barn event space
  • A newly built two story event space
  • A one-of-a-kind waterfall that runs through the front of the property
  • Original stonewalls surrounding the entrance of the estate
  • Lush well-kept greenery throughout the property
  • A pool and connected cabana
  • Stunning views of the valley and surrounding mountains

These unique details and more are why 50ACF hosts numerous wedding ceremonies and receptions, birthday parties, corporate outings, retirement parties, family celebrations, and other gatherings every year.

Sitting between Dallas PA and Beaumont PA on Route 309 North at the corner of Falls Hill Road, 50 Acre Farm Estate offers a perfect blend of rustic style and convenience for your special occasion. With two separate event spaces each capable of hosting 120 – 200 guests and x different event packages, we have options every type of event and budget.

About the Farm:

The 50 Acre Farm Estate (WWW.50ACF.com), established in the mid-1800s is a more than one-hundred-and-fifty-year-old farm with waterfalls and creeks situated on 50 private acres. Before it was purchased by the current owner of the property, it was a local dairy farm that supplied much of the surrounding region for over a century. This historic farmstead is registered with the National Register Program, Bureau for Historic Preservation in Harrisburg PA. First mentioned in the Civil War Draft Registration in 1863, the Farm is a historic American protected asset.

Introduction:

Perfect for weddings, birthday parties, corporate gatherings, and other special events, this unique property offers stunning views of the endless mountains along with the waterfall and stream located on the property. The owner’s main home is a Palladio style inspired residency built with field stone and local materials to match the landscape. It is located at the top of the property, overlooking the fifty-acre farm.

In general, the environment of the entire property is cozy and scenic. A perfect setting for entertaining. It is a spectacular private estate with great panoramic views of the countryside. Many of those who visit the estate compare the views to those seen in Tuscany, Italy.

This classic and stylish private estate is kept as a farm. The land and certain buildings are used to occasionally grow crops and raise livestock. However, it is well maintained and has a long driveway from the street level up the top of the hill. The waterfalls are on the right side of the private street entrance to the property. On top of the property, you overlook the fabulous scenery of the Back Mountains.

The entire estate was created to make every guest feel at home so they can chat, drink, and have fun while enjoying your special day. The outside landscape is great in all four seasons. From overgrown foliage from trees, bushes, and vines to old grown Rhododendron, Trumpet flowers, and wild climbers, the native plants make the foliage a part of the unique space.

While the native greenery is an important part of the estate, the waterfalls located at the front of the property are simply incredible and the most popular spot for guest photos.

The Barn can be used for an additional rental fee. At the entrance of the property, the open rustic space can be setup as a bar for welcome beverages and snacks.
The waterfalls can be used for group or individual pictures.
