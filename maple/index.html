About the Farm:

The Estate (WWW.50ACF.com) Estate, established in mid-1800 is a more than one and half century old farm with waterfalls and creeks situated on 50 private acres. It was back in the days used as a dairy farm. This historic Farmstead from 1865 is registered with the National Register Program, Bureau for Historic Preservation in Harrisburg PA. First mention in the Civil War Draft Registration in 1863. The Farm an historic American protected asset.

Introduction:

Perfect for weddings, corporate or special events with stunning views of the Endless Mountain. The owners Main house is a Palladio style inspired residence build with field stone with and local materials to match the landscape. It is located at the top of the property, overlooking the fifty-acre farm.

In general, the environment of the entire property is cozy and scenic. A perfect setting for entertaining. It is a spectacular private estate with great panoramic views. Many visitors and friends compare the views to the Tuscany in Italy.

This classic and stylish private estate is kept as an Farm. The land and its buildings areused to grow crops or raise livestock. It is well maintained and has a long driveway from the street level up the top of the hill. The waterfalls are on the right side of the private street entrance to the property. On top of the property you overlook the fabulous scenery from the Back Mountains.

This entire place holds all guests together for a nice chat and drink to having fun. A nice spot in this cozy atmosphere. Outside the Landscaping is great in all four seasons, overgrown foliage from trees, bushes and vines. The waterfalls during the whole year are incredible. Especially in the rain seasons they are huge and nobody want miss them. A perfect place for taking picture.
