We Are The Investment
Fund Experts

The most difficult questions that no one else can answer eventually find their way to us: accounting, administration, taxation, tax structuring/structures and other matters related to investment funds, their management companies, or general partners, are just some of the topics we are consulted on/asked about. Clients approach our firm through the recommendations of colleagues, legal advisors, and auditors who have first-hand knowledge of our expertise and reputation in the fund market.

We Are Trusted Advisors

Some of our clients have been with us for over 10 years and value our judgement when making any major decisions. More often than not, our opinion is accepted as the "final word".

We See The Big Picture

And we stay two steps ahead — foreseeing anything that might ever go wrong — while putting the right measures in place to prevent future problems even before the fund manager becomes aware of a potential risk.

Industry
