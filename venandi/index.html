HEALTH, SAFETY, AND ENVIRONMENT (HSE)

HSE is more than an issue of compliance, it is fundamental to the way in which we operate.

The core of our commitment is a comprehensive HSE Management System which is followed by our staff and a requirement for any contractors and sub-contractors we work with. We expect 100% compliance with legal and safety procedures. All contractors are required to manage their activities in line with our Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) Policy and Commitment. Accountability for HSE management is clearly defined between our company and our contractor through all stages of their work.

Sanctions:

  1. We comply with all applicable sanction laws and regulation
  2. All new counterparties must be screened as part of the KYC process for any sanction-related issues.
  3. Under no circumstances should we or our subcontractors seek to structure transactions or amend documentation in order to circumvent any applicable sanction or trade restriction or to otherwise attempt to disguise the true nature of a transaction
