Our Services

SmartCity 2030 offers local services within four key sectors: government, business, civil society, and academia.
An overview of our services within these sectors include:

Government

  • Financing
  • Policy formation
  • Innovation
  • Support
  • Advisory services

Business

  • Entrepreneurial venturing
  • Product and service development

Civil Society

  • Collaboration platform
  • Legitimating link competence and process development
  • Carrying individual and structural perspectives

Academia

  • Research and development
  • Education
  • Incubators
  • Spin-offs
Our work within these sectors will often comingle in our effort to build a strong network and community in the Baltimore Washington Corridor.
