Our Purpose

SmartCity2030 is dedicated to building a first-in-class S.T.E.A.M. (Science Technology Math Engineering Arts Mathematics) media network in the Baltimore Washington Corridor. We hone in on topical issues relating to smart grid infrastructure, 5G, and IoT programs including:

  • Cybersecurity
  • Supply-chain logistics security
  • Creative economy networking

The key purpose of Smart City 2030 is to become the highest level S.T.E.A.M. network in the Baltimore Washington Corridor. Our objective is to foster an ecological, ethical economic world view.

Developing a “Smart Grid” Business Development Network

Locally, our program aims to build a “Smart Grid” business development network in the Baltimore Washington Corridor. We’ll connect with organizations looking to collaborate with global trends and offer support for a local creative economy “Ideas Festival”.

These organizations will include both environmental corporations and the fundamental B and C tier corporations, trade unions, vocational training centers, and commercial real estate networks.

Growing a Cybervilliage in The Hard Science Community

As a digital media platform, we’re also committed to building a cybervillage of environmental investors, medical technologists, and other professionals operating in the hard science community around College Park, National Landing, Washington DC, and Baltimore.
