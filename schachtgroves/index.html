Premium Indian River Citrus Gifts Since 1950

We are a fourth generation citrus family, and we pride ourselves on growing a consistently delicious variety of citrus from our own groves in the renowed growing Indian River growing district. We sell our citrus locally, as well as ship it acround the country.

We operate a Farm Store for the local residents of Indian River County, FL. Our store features local produce, honey, eggs, and meat. We also offer unique gifts and locally handcrafted items.
