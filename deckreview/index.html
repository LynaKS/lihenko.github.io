Questions I Often Get Asked:

Are you really doing this for free?

Yes, it’s 100% free. There’s an opportunity for you to make a voluntary contribution for my time - it’s not necessary, but it is appreciated.

Why are you doing this?

I want to help. I’ve seen so many great ideas and teams that fall short because the decks fail to present and illustrate key items that investors want to know. Ultimately, I want to see more founders succeed in raising capital.

I also think that this will be a great way to meet entrepreneurs and their supporters in Brazil and LATAM while adding value to the region’s entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Why only Brazil?

Anyone from anywhere can submit their presentation for a review. However, I’m extremely interested in this region as I feel it represents a great opportunity for investment – an opportunity that may rival the success seen in the USA’s key entrepreneurial markets.

Can I get more help from you?

Will you steal my idea?

No, I will not.

Will you share my information or sell it to service providers?

No. I’m not doing this to generate leads for other services. I truly hope that my feedback will help, and I get the chance to meet great founders and members of the entrepreneurial ecosystem.

The rest will take care of itself.

What if I do not like or agree with your feedback?

That’s ok - it is free, remember? These are just my opinions and perspectives, not the law. Getting feedback from many stakeholders is an important part of the process and I’m trying to make some of that feedback more accessible.

Will you invest in my business?

It’s possible, but unlikely. Investing in startups isn’t my goal for this service.
